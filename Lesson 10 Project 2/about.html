About Us

M. D. A Freight, Inc Trucking is one of the most respected truckload carriers in the trucking industry. M.D.A is considered to be one of the safest and most reliable large truckload carriers in North America. M.D.A offers local, regional and trans-continental truckload services within the United States and to and from Canada including temperature control and regional heavy haul service. With a reliable network of partner carriers, M.D.A also has the ability to provide additional truck capacity to customers without sacrificing service value. M.D.A also offers customized transportation solutions like customer dedicated fleets and on-site transportation management to some of North America's most successful companies. Since its inception, M.D.A Trucking has built its reputation on providing innovative transportation solutions for customers. The company has always focused on doing business with successful companies and building meaningful relationships. These relationships have helped enable M.D.A to deliver consistent and evolving transportation value. In addition, M.D.A continues to adopt advanced technology to improve efficiencies and relies on a driving force and supporting personnel that is truly best in class.

Ownership

M.D.A Freight was Founded in 1946 with just two trucks, Jay started out hauling beer from Atlanta, GA to Birmingham, AL for his first customer, the Lucky Lager Brewery. In 1984, Jay sold the company to his son Larry and his wife Virginia. Larry and Virginia's children, Steve and Scott became actively involved in the company in the early 90's. As third generation leaders and M.D.A Trucking shareholders, Steve and Scott helped build the company into an industry leading, 2000 truck carrier. M.D.A Trucking was sold to Heartland Express in November of 2013. Today, Steve and Scott are part of the leadership group of Heartland Express. Additionally, Larry was invited to become a member of the Heartland Board of Directors and the Gordon Family collectively owns a significant amount of Heartland Express Stock (HTLD).
